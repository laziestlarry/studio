# **App Name**: BizOp Navigator

## Core Features:

- Opportunity Identifier: Employ AI tool to sift through online data, identifying promising business opportunities with minimal manual oversight. The AI assesses market gaps and emerging trends to pinpoint ventures with high potential.
- Market Analysis: Leverage AI to analyze selected market opportunities. This includes demand forecasting, competitive landscape analysis, and potential revenue projections, providing users with data-driven insights.
- Automated Strategy Builder: Based on market analysis, this AI tool generates a comprehensive business strategy including marketing tactics, operational workflows, and financial forecasts.
- AI Task Prioritization: Using AI, the tool helps prioritize which online business venture to develop or begin. These could include, but not limited to: side-hustles, passive income generators, freelance performance portals, drop shipping e-commerce traders, and social media management.
- Interactive Dashboard: Visualize AI-driven insights through charts, graphs, and customizable dashboards, enhancing decision-making. It provides clear metrics on profitability and risk assessments.
- Guidance Prompts: This feature distills business advice into easy to read and follow guidance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) to evoke trust, growth, and stability, resonating with financial and business contexts.
- Background color: Soft, desaturated light teal (#E0F8F8), maintaining the thematic color harmony.
- Accent color: Warm yellow-orange (#FFB347) to highlight key opportunities and call to actions.
- Body text: 'Inter' sans-serif for clean readability of reports; Headline text: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif for a clear techy style.
- Icons should be simple, modern, and related to data analysis, finance, and business strategy. They should have outlines, so that their line weight remains consistent at all sizes.
- Ensure the layout is clean and structured with a focus on data presentation and ease of navigation. Incorporate modular blocks for each identified opportunity.
- Subtle animations when loading data or transitioning between opportunities to enhance user engagement without distraction.
